Extension of biodivine aeon desktop aplication, which will be responsible for performance of control task.

The app is build on Tauri same as the original biodivine aeon desktop app.

Frontend:
-import and export of calculated control data together with data about boolean network (needed extension of the .aeon format)
-input of control parameters (perturbable variables, phenotype, size limit, robustness limit)
-filtering of control data (colours, cardinality, size, ranks)
-navigation in the control data through table of data
-navigation in the control data through grafical interface
-visualization of data about control in the form of graphs
-generation of parametererized boolean network deppending on chosen perturbation/colour
